Hi,
my name is Hadif Mufti. I am currently a senior at the International School of Bellevue. As a tradition, all graduating seniors must complete a Senior Project based off the topic of their choice, with guidance from mentors and staff. For my project, I wanted to explore internet addiction as I have seen many peers struggle with balancing responsibilities like school work with video game and social media use in particular.

I wanted to be realistic, there was no way I could cure internet addiction on my own with 0 knowledge on addiction. So, I thought a better idea would be to create a website with information about internet addiction and a quiz to see if people exhibit symptoms of internet addiction, so they can get help.

For my project, I needed to learn about addiction so I researched the psychology of it including watching podcasts, readings, and a tour at reSTART. With this knowledge, I then learned web development through online research including the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I created this website from scratch and without the use of third party programs that guide you through the process of making a website.

Although, it must be stated that since I am NOT an addiction professional I cannot diagnose anyone. Although, the information in my website is all from profesionals and is cited.
